Welcome to a little about me

I grew up mostly in Texas. I miss the mild winters and awesome thunderstorms.
I spent my high school years in Virgina at the Virginia School of the Arts. I was a serious dancer for a while and danced professionally for about 4 years after I graduated.
I went to college, got married, went to law school, and we had 6 children.
My favorite pastimes are reading, writing, and going on adventures with my family.
Not right now, but someday I want a new house and a new car. For now, I've got the best husband.
I am completely converted to a whole food plant based way of life. That means I don't eat any animal products at all and try to eat only food that's in it's original packaging. Like I don't drink apple juice, but I eat apples.
I'd love to get a book published someday. I'm learning to code. I don't know if I love it yet.
